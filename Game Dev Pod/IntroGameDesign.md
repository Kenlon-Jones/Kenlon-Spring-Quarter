What is Game Design? 
Game Design is the process of shaping the rules, systems, mechanics, challenges, goals, and player experience that define a game.

What is the MDA framework? What are its parts? Define each.
MDA Framework is a powerful tool we use to understand and analyze game design. The 3 parts are mechanics, dynamics, and aesthetics. Mechanics define core rules, systes, and actions players can do in-game. Dynamics is the gameplay that comes from players interacting with the game's mechanics. Aesthetics are the way the game feels to a player and their emotional response to the gameplay.
